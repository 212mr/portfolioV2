TIMEORANGE

- an app to know your self -


TIMEORANGE is an mobile phone app to track screen time usage. It sets budget of daily screen time spending and reminds the user not to over use mobile phone. It is an individual UX/UI project from problem research to product prototypes. Then I coded it with JQuery mobile UI.

PROBLEM


Smartphone Overuse

Smartphone overuse (mobil phone addiction or problem mobile phone use) is a dependence syndrome seen among certain mobile phone users. Some mobile phone users exhibit problematic behaviors related to substance use disorders.
