SMARTDELIVERY

- an app to manage your packages -


SmartDelivery is an mobile app to summarize online shopping package delivery status by auto detecting emails.

It is also an IoT (Internet of Things) system with a camera to view the front door and a remote control door-locker.

This is an individual user experiment project started from idea exploration to user experience research. After many rounds of user tests with wireframes, I made with interface prototypes.

DESIGN TOOLS:
Sketch / Adobe XD / Adobe Photoshop
