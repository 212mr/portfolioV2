Culture Exploration

The timeline of United States inventions (1890–1945) encompasses the ingenuity and innovative advancements of the United States within a historical context, dating from the Progressive Era to the end of World War II, which have been achieved by inventors who are either native-born or naturalized citizens of the United States.[1] Advances in technology led to the age of electricity and many homes in America, especially in the industrialized cities, were powered by electricity. Access to electricity from the 1920's provided Americans with the power required to run new labor-saving devices for a better life.
